Welcome to the PREMIUM_EU Dashboard


Curious about how mobility is reshaping your region or how to make it work in your favour? You’re in the right place.

This dashboard helps policymakers understand demographic trends, regional development patterns, and policy solutions.

Here, you’ll find tailored insights into who’s moving, why they move, and what it means for regional development.

Explore your region’s mobility and development profile, compare with others, and discover real policy examples that tackle brain drain, attract newcomers, and boost resilience.

Start by selecting your region or dive into the data and stories that matter to you.
